# Free Health Services — City of Koronadal

The City Government of Koronadal offers free **check-ups, basic medicines, and vaccines** through the City Health Office (CHO), barangay health stations, and partner programs.

---

## 1. Free Medical Check-ups

| Service              | Where                                         | Schedule                                 | Requirements                                              |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| General consultation | City Health Office / Barangay Health Stations | Weekday clinic hours (varies by station) | Individual Treatment Record (ITR) — issued on first visit |

---

## 2. Free Basic Medicines

| Service              | Where                    | Schedule             | Requirements                                                 |
| -------------------- | ------------------------ | -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Free basic medicines | City Health Office (CHO) | Weekday office hours | Doctor's prescription and/or referral from a hospital/clinic |

> Availability of specific medicines depends on current stock and DOH program supply. Confirm with the CHO before going.

---

## 3. Free Vaccines and Immunization

| Beneficiary       | Vaccine/Service                                          | Where                    | Schedule                                       | Requirements                                              |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Children          | BCG, Pentavalent, OPV/IPV, MMR, Hepatitis B, PCV13 (EPI) | Barangay Health Stations | On the posted immunization day at your station | ECCD card (first-timers can get one onsite)               |
| Pregnant women    | Tetanus Toxoid vaccine and supplements                   | Barangay Health Stations | During prenatal services                       | Proof of pregnancy, prenatal record                       |
| Animal bite cases | Anti-rabies vaccine (per DOH program)                    | City Health Office (CHO) | Clinic hours                                   | Proof of Koronadal residency (e.g., barangay certificate) |

> For animal-bite care and rabies concerns, the **City Veterinary Office** (Head: Charlemagne S. Calo) also assists with animal-related public health matters. Coordinate through the CHO or City Hall trunkline.

---

## How to Avail

1. **Check-ups** — go to your barangay health station or the City Health Office; first-timers will be issued an ITR.
2. **Medicines** — bring a doctor's prescription or referral and submit it at the CHO.
3. **Child immunization** — visit your barangay health station on the posted immunization day with the ECCD card.
4. **Pregnancy services** — attend prenatal visits at the barangay health station for the free vaccine and supplements.
5. **Anti-rabies** — report to the City Health Office; bring proof of residency.
