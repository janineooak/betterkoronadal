# Find a Job Through PESO — City of Koronadal

Looking for work in Koronadal or nearby towns? The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) helps residents register as jobseekers, get matched to employers, and receive a referral letter for possible employment. PESO also assists local companies in running on-site Local Recruitment Activities (job fairs) where you can apply directly.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Register at PESO

- Go to PESO and ask for the National Manpower Registration System (NMRS) Application Form.
- Fill it out completely. PESO staff will guide you.

### Step 2: Submit your documents

- Hand in your completed NMRS form along with your other requirements (ID, application letter, resume, school records, diploma, NBI/Police Clearance).
- Staff will check your documents for completeness and conduct a short preliminary interview.

### Step 3: Pay the fees

- PESO will issue an order of payment. Proceed to the City Treasurer's Office to pay the Peso Referral fee (PHP 50) and Occupational Tax ID (PHP 200).
- Keep the Official Receipt issued upon payment.

### Step 4: Return for the final interview

- Bring your receipt back to PESO for a final interview with the PESO Manager or authorized personnel.

### Step 5: Claim your PESO referral

- PESO prepares and releases your referral letter, which you present to the company you are referred to.

### For local job fairs (Local Recruitment Activity)

- Watch for PESO-organized Local Recruitment Activities (LRAs). Local employers leave job orders with PESO and conduct on-site recruitment, so you can apply directly during the event.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Filled-up National Manpower Registration System (NMRS) Application Form (from PESO)
- [ ] Government-issued ID (1 original for verification, 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Application letter (1 original, 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Resume or Bio-Data (1 original, 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Transcript of Records (TOR) or School Card (1 original, 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Diploma (1 original, 1 photocopy)
- [ ] NBI / Police Clearance (1 original, 1 photocopy)

### Eligibility

- You must be a bona fide resident of the City of Koronadal.

## Fees

For an employment referral, the charter lists:

- Peso Referral — PHP 50
- Occupational Tax ID — PHP 200
- **Total: PHP 250**, paid at the City Treasurer's Office.

Attending a Local Recruitment Activity (job fair) as a jobseeker is free.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I have to pay just to register as a jobseeker?**
A: The charter lists the referral process with a total of PHP 250 (PHP 50 Peso Referral + PHP 200 Occupational Tax ID). When in doubt, ask PESO which fees apply to your situation before paying.

**Q: Can PESO guarantee me a job?**
A: No. PESO provides employment information, job matching, and a referral letter to a company for possible employment. The hiring decision rests with the employer.

**Q: I don't have my NBI or Police Clearance yet. What do I do?**
A: Secure your NBI Clearance from the NBI and your Police Clearance from the City Police Station before completing your application, as these are part of the checklist.

**Q: How can I join a job fair?**
A: PESO organizes Local Recruitment Activities with local employers. Follow the City Government of Koronadal Facebook page or ask PESO for the next scheduled event.
