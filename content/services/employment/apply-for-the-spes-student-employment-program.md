# Apply for the SPES Student Employment Program — City of Koronadal

The Special Program for the Employment of Students (SPES) is DOLE's youth employment-bridging program, facilitated locally by the Public Employment Service Office (PESO). It provides temporary employment to poor but deserving students, out-of-school youth, and dependents of displaced workers to help them stay in school.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Get and submit your application

- Go to PESO, ask for the SPES Application Form, and fill it out.
- Submit it together with your other documents. Staff will check them for completeness.

### Step 2: Log in to the DOLE SPES portal

- PESO will assist you in logging in to the DOLE SPES portal and uploading your requirements.
- Wait for your examination schedule, which PESO announces via text, email, or phone call.

### Step 3: Take the examination

- Return to PESO on your scheduled date to take the exam (allow about 2 hours).
- PESO checks the exam, posts the results, and announces the schedule of final interviews for those who pass.

### Step 4: Final selection

- PESO submits the shortlist of qualified applicants to the Office of the Mayor for final selection, then submits the final list of beneficiaries to DOLE-SCFO and requests the Notice to Proceed from DOLE.

### Step 5: Attend orientation and sign your contract

- Attend the orientation and sign your SPES contract with PESO. You will then be assigned to a city department or partner establishment.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Original Income Tax Return (ITR) or Certificate of Indigency (from BIR / Barangay Hall / DSWD)
- [ ] Form 138 / Transcript of Records (TOR) / latest grade report (from your school)
- [ ] PSA Birth Certificate (1 original, 1 photocopy)
- [ ] School ID (1 original)
- [ ] Filled-up SPES Application Form (1 original, from PESO)

### Eligibility

- You must be 15–30 years old.
- You must be a high school graduating student, out-of-school youth, college student/drop-out, or a dependent of a displaced worker.
- Your parents' combined after-tax income must not exceed the latest threshold level for a family of six.

---

## Fees

None — this service is free.

---

## Good to Know

- Work duration is a minimum of 10 days but not more than 72 days.
- Beneficiaries are entitled to the minimum wage: 60% is paid by the City Government of Koronadal and 40% by the Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE).
- Beneficiaries are assigned to different city government departments and partner agencies or business establishments.
- The program is conducted every year, often during summer and/or Christmas vacation.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How much will I earn?**
A: SPES beneficiaries receive the minimum wage. The City Government pays 60% and DOLE pays 40%.

**Q: How long is the job?**
A: A minimum of 10 days and a maximum of 72 days.

**Q: Is there a fee to apply?**
A: No. SPES is free.

**Q: Do I need to pass a test?**
A: Yes. After registering through the DOLE SPES portal, you take an examination at PESO. Those who pass move on to a final interview and selection.

**Q: Who decides who gets selected?**
A: PESO submits a shortlist of qualified applicants to the Office of the Mayor for final selection.
