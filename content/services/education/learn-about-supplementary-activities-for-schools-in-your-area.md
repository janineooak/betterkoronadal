# Learn About Supplementary Activities for Schools in Your Area — City of Koronadal

This guide shows where to find **clubs, student organizations, competitions, service groups, and enrichment programs** available to learners in the City of Koronadal.

---

## Where to Look First (Local)

1. **Your school's official page or Facebook** — most clubs post tryout and enlistment dates there.
2. **DepEd Koronadal City Division** — division-wide contests, trainings, and circulars. DepEd is a national agency; check your Division office or your school for official memos and contacts.
3. **City facilities** — the **Koronadal City Library** offers study space and reference services for students. Confirm its location, hours, and available services through the City Hall trunkline, (083) 228-6095.

## Academic & Interest Clubs (examples you'll commonly find)

- **STEM/Math/Science clubs** — science fairs, investigatory projects, robotics/ICT showcases
- **Araling Panlipunan & Social Science clubs** — quiz bowls, heritage walks, civic advocacy
- **MAPEH / Arts, Music, Dance & Theater** — talent showcases, chorale, dance troupe, theater guild
- **TLE/ICT clubs** — coding basics, multimedia, entrepreneurship pop-ups
- **Sports teams** — tryouts announced by the school's MAPEH/sports coordinator (division meets posted by DepEd)

> Tip: Most schools post **club enlistment** in the first month of classes and **tryout notices** before division meets. Keep an eye on your school's channels.

## Enrichment & Alternative Pathways

- **ALS (Alternative Learning System)** — if you're out-of-school youth or need flexible learning. Ask the DepEd Koronadal City Division office for the ALS focal persons in your area.
- **City Library Programs** — study support and reference services at the Koronadal City Library.
- **Youth, Environment & Disaster Readiness** — often led by YES-O, Red Cross Youth, and school DRRM coordinators.

---

## How to Join (Quick Steps)

1. **Scan your school's bulletin or Facebook** for club fair dates, sign-up links, or adviser contacts.
2. **Prepare simple requirements** (school ID, and a parent consent form if needed).
3. **Attend the orientation or tryouts** and note the meeting schedules.
4. **Commit to service hours or outputs** (events, training, competitions).
5. **Track division-level announcements** via DepEd Koronadal City Division for contests and training invitations.

---

## Parent/Guardian Pointers

- Ask advisers about **safety protocols**, **meeting times**, and **fees (if any)**. Many activities are free or school-supported.
- Balance: set a weekly schedule so academics and extracurriculars both thrive.
- Encourage roles that build **leadership and documentation** (such as secretary, treasurer, or logistics head).
