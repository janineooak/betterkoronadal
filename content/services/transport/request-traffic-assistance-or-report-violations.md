# Request Traffic Assistance or Report Violations — City of Koronadal

The **City Traffic Management Office (CTMO)**, headed by Joy L. Placer, helps keep traffic flowing safely across Koronadal. This guide covers three services: requesting traffic assistance for events and motorcades, filing complaints against tricycle drivers or traffic enforcers, and requesting an orientation seminar on traffic rules and regulations.

## How to Apply

### Request for Traffic Assistance (motorcades, parades, burial assistance, events)

#### Step 1: Submit your requirements

- Bring the Special Permit issued by the City Administrator and your route plan to the CTMO. Staff check them for correctness and completeness, and discuss the route plan details with you.

#### Step 2: Wait for the detail order

- The CTMO prepares and signs a detail order for your scheduled event, then convenes the concerned traffic personnel beforehand to give instructions.

### Filing of Complaints (against tricycle drivers, traffic enforcers, etc.)

#### Step 1: Lodge the complaint

- Report the incident to the CTMO. Staff record the complaint and interview you.
- If the respondent is a tricycle driver, the CTMO checks its file of registered tricycle drivers, prepares a summons, and coordinates with the Koronadal Radio Operation Work Net (KROWN) to transmit the complaint to traffic enforcers.

#### Step 2: Settlement

- Both parties are informed of the settlement schedule. The CTMO interrogates both sides and mediates an amicable settlement. Once settled, both parties sign the logbook to manifest their intention to settle.

### Request for an Orientation Seminar on Traffic Rules and Regulations

#### Step 1: Submit an approved request letter

- A TODA submits a request letter approved by the City Mayor. The CTMO checks the details and sets a schedule for the seminar.

#### Step 2: Coordinate on materials

- Coordinate with the CTMO on the materials needed. Traffic personnel brainstorm and prepare.

#### Step 3: Attend the seminar

- The CTMO facilitates the orientation/seminar on the agreed schedule and issues a certificate of completion to the participating TODA.

## Requirements

### For Request for Traffic Assistance

- [ ] Special Permit issued by the City Administrator (1 original, 1 photocopy) — secured from the City Administrator's Office
- [ ] Route plan (1 original)

### For Filing of Complaints

- [ ] Complete details about the complaint
- [ ] Driver's license (1 original, for verification purposes only)

### For Orientation Seminar

- [ ] Request letter approved by the City Mayor (1 original, 1 photocopy)

### Eligibility

- Traffic assistance and filing of complaints: open to all.
- Orientation seminar: Tricycle Operators and Drivers Associations (TODA) within the City of Koronadal.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I'm organizing a motorcade/parade. What do I need first?**
A: A Special Permit issued by the City Administrator, plus a route plan. Bring both to the CTMO so they can prepare a detail order and assign traffic personnel.

**Q: A tricycle driver overcharged or mistreated me. How do I complain?**
A: Lodge the complaint at the CTMO with the complete details and present the driver's license (for verification only). The CTMO will summon the respondent and mediate a settlement between both parties.

**Q: Is there a fee to file a complaint or request a seminar?**
A: No. These services are free of charge.

**Q: Who can request a traffic rules seminar?**
A: A Tricycle Operators and Drivers Association (TODA) within Koronadal, through a request letter approved by the City Mayor. Participants receive a certificate of completion.
