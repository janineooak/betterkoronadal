# Register a Vehicle and Pay Terminal Fees — City of Koronadal

Public utility vehicles (PUVs) that use the City of Koronadal Integrated Transport Terminal Complex (CKITTC) must register and pay terminal fees per entry. These services are managed by the **City Economic Enterprise Development Office (CEEDO) – Operation of Terminal**, headed by Kirbie Mae Pacinabao-Obando. This guide covers registering your PUV and paying the terminal fees.

## How to Apply

### Registering a Public Utility Vehicle in the City Terminal

#### Step 1: Submit your requirements

- Bring your requirements to the City Terminal Management Office (CKITTC Administration Office). Staff receive them and check for completeness and authenticity.

#### Step 2: Get your PUV Registration / Gate Pass

- The office issues your PUV Registration / Gate Pass.

#### Step 3: Affix the gate pass

- The terminal staff affix the PUV Registration Gate Pass to the right side of the vehicle's windshield glass.

### Paying of Terminal Fees (per entry)

#### Step 1: Present your registration

- Present your valid PUV Registration to the Van Ticket Booth. Staff check its validity and issue a queuing number.

#### Step 2: Pay the cash ticket

- Pay the corresponding fee for your vehicle type at the Van Ticket Booth. Staff issue a cash ticket noting the date and time of the transaction on the back.

#### Step 3: Present the cash ticket at the exit

- Present the cash ticket at the Gate 4 Exit Checker, who marks it and returns it to you.

#### Step 4: Present at the terminal outpost

- Present the cash ticket at the designated Terminal Outpost for a final compliance check.

## Requirements

### For Registering a PUV

- [ ] Duly filled-out Individual PUV Application Form (1 original, 1 photocopy) — from the CKITTC Administration Office
- [ ] Business/Mayor's Permit for transport operation (1 photocopy) — from the Business Permit Licensing Office where the business is registered
- [ ] LTFRB Franchise (1 Certified True Copy) — from LTFRB R-12
- [ ] Left, front, right, and back photos of the vehicle for registration (1 of each)
- [ ] Driver's license of all designated drivers (1 photocopy each) — from LTO

### For Paying Terminal Fees

- [ ] PUV Registration Sticker / Gate Pass (1 original)

### Eligibility

- PUV registration: operators of public transport vehicles with a franchise that includes the City of Koronadal in their approved route.
- Paying terminal fees: drivers, conductors, and operators of registered Koronadal public transport vehicles.

## Fees

**Registering a PUV in the City Terminal**

- None.

**Paying of Terminal Fees** (per entry, varies by vehicle type)

- Bus: PhP 70.00 per entry
- Shuttle Van: PhP 30.00 per entry
- Jeepney: PhP 20.00 per entry
- Multicab: PhP 15.00 per entry
- Tricycle: PhP 10.00 per day

These fees are covered by Ordinance No. 9, Series of 2018 (the ordinance setting the guidelines for the operation and management of the CKITTC).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does it cost anything to register my PUV at the terminal?**
A: No. Registering a PUV in the City Terminal has no fee. You only pay the terminal fee per entry afterward.

**Q: How much is the terminal fee?**
A: It depends on the vehicle type: Bus PhP 70, Shuttle Van PhP 30, Jeepney PhP 20, and Multicab PhP 15 per entry; tricycles pay PhP 10 per day.

**Q: Where do I put the gate pass?**
A: Terminal staff affix the PUV Registration Gate Pass to the right side of your vehicle's windshield glass.

**Q: Who can register a PUV here?**
A: Operators of public transport vehicles whose franchise includes the City of Koronadal in their approved route.
