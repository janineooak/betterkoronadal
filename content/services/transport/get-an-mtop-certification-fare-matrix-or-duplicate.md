# Get an MTOP Certification, Fare Matrix, or Duplicate Copy — City of Koronadal

Need a certification about your tricycle franchise, a replacement copy of your approved MTOP or fare matrix, or data about MTOPs for research? The **City Mayor's Office – Franchising Section** can issue these documents. This guide covers MTOP certifications, duplicate copies of approved documents, and requests for MTOP-related data.

## How to Apply

### MTOP Certification

#### Step 1: Secure and fill out the request form

- Staff assess your eligibility and provide the request form for you to fill out.

#### Step 2: Submit your documents

- Submit the accomplished request form with your requirements. Staff check them and process the request, which is then signed by the City Mayor.

#### Step 3: Pay the fees

- An order of payment is issued. Pay at the City Treasurer's Office and keep your Official Receipt.

#### Step 4: Claim your certification

- Return to the Franchising Section and claim your MTOP certification.

### Duplicate Copy of Approved MTOP, Fare Matrix, or Notice of Hearing

#### Step 1: Submit documentary requirements

- Submit a request form stating your purpose, along with the tricycle OR/CR (and a Special Power of Attorney or authorization letter if you are a representative). Staff prepare the requested copy and endorse it to the Franchising Officer III for approval.

#### Step 2: Pay the fees

- Pay the corresponding fee at the City Treasurer's Office and keep your Official Receipt.

#### Step 3: Claim the requested copy

- Return to the Franchising Section and claim your document.

### Requesting Data Relative to MTOP

#### Step 1: Submit your request

- Submit an approved letter request addressed to the City Mayor, plus a government-issued ID. Staff prepare the requested data or documents (this can take up to 6 days, as it is a Complex transaction).

#### Step 2: Pay the fees

- Pay at the City Treasurer's Office and keep your Official Receipt.

#### Step 3: Claim the data/documents

- Present your Official Receipt at the Franchising Section and claim the requested data or documents.

## Requirements

### For MTOP Certification

- [ ] Accomplished request form
- [ ] Latest approved MTOP (1 original or 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Duly registered OR and CR of tricycle ownership (1 original or 1 photocopy)

### For Duplicate Copies (Approved MTOP, Fare Matrix, Notice of Hearing, etc.)

- [ ] Request form stating the purpose (1 original)
- [ ] Duly registered OR and CR of tricycle ownership (1 original or 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Special Power of Attorney or authorization letter if you are a representative of the owner (1 original or 1 photocopy)

### For Requesting Data Relative to MTOP

- [ ] Approved letter request addressed to the City Mayor (2 originals)
- [ ] Government-issued ID (1 original, 1 photocopy)

### Eligibility

- MTOP certifications: open to all.
- Duplicate copies: Motorized Tricycle Operator's Permit holders.
- Data requests: open to all (anyone needing MTOP data for research purposes).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I lost my fare matrix / Notice of Hearing. Can I get a replacement?**
A: Yes. Request a duplicate copy at the Franchising Section. A lost fare matrix or lost Notice of Hearing each carry a PhP 100.00 fee.

**Q: Can someone else process the duplicate on my behalf?**
A: Yes, but they must bring a Special Power of Attorney or an authorization letter, in addition to the other requirements.

**Q: Why does the data request take so long?**
A: Requesting MTOP data is classified as a Complex transaction. Preparing the requested data/documents can take up to 6 days, plus a few minutes for the rest of the steps.

**Q: Who can request MTOP certifications or data?**
A: Both are open to all. Duplicate copies, however, are for MTOP holders (or their authorized representatives).
