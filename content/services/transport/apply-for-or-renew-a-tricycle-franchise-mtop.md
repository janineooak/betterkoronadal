# Apply for or Renew a Tricycle Franchise (MTOP) — City of Koronadal

If you own or operate a tricycle-for-hire in Koronadal, you need a Motorized Tricycle Operator's Permit (MTOP). This guide covers applying for a new permit, renewing, substituting a unit, transferring a franchise, complying with the yearly inspection and fees, and dropping a permit. All of these are handled by the **City Mayor's Office – Franchising Section** (Business Permit, Licensing and Franchising Division).

## How to Apply

### New, Renewal, Unit Substitution, or Transfer of Franchise

> **Renew early.** Applications for renewal must be filed **one (1) month before the expiration date**. Filing less than a month before expiry is considered late and is subject to penalties.

#### Step 1: Get the petition form and requirements

- Go to the Franchising Section. Staff will assess your eligibility and give you a Petition Form and the list of requirements.

#### Step 2: Have the petition notarized, then submit your documents

- Fill out the petition and have it notarized by a Notary Public.
- Submit the duly notarized petition with your documentary requirements. Staff check them for completeness and authenticity.

#### Step 3: Pay the fees at the City Treasurer's Office

- The Franchising Section issues an order of payment. Pay at the City Treasurer's Office and keep your Official Receipt.

#### Step 4: Get your Notice of Hearing and attend the public hearing

- Present your receipt and secure the claim stub for the Notice of Hearing.
- Return on the scheduled date to claim the Notice of Hearing from the Sangguniang Panlungsod Committee on Transportation, and attend the public hearing. Your application then goes to the Sangguniang Panlungsod for evaluation and legislative action.

#### Step 5: Claim your approved MTOP

- Once approved, return to the Franchising Section, attend the MTOP orientation, present your Official Receipt, and claim your approved MTOP. Display it on the tricycle as proof of legitimate operation within the city.

### Annual MTOP Compliance (every first quarter)

#### Step 1: Submit your inspection slip

- Go to the Franchising Section and submit your inspection slip (with remarks "PASSED"), along with your latest approved MTOP and the OR/CR of the tricycle. Staff evaluate the status of your MTOP and issue an order of payment.

#### Step 2: Pay the annual fees

- Pay at the City Treasurer's Office and keep your Official Receipt.

#### Step 3: Claim your annual sticker

- Return to the Franchising Section, present your receipt, and claim the annual sticker.

### Dropping an MTOP (ceasing operation)

#### Step 1: Submit requirements for assessment

- Submit your Letter of Intent, latest approved MTOP, and the OR/CR of the tricycle. Note: any outstanding fees and charges must be settled in full at the City Treasurer's Office.

#### Step 2: Pay the corresponding fees

- Pay at the City Treasurer's Office and keep your Official Receipt.

#### Step 3: Endorsement and approval

- The application is endorsed to the Sangguniang Panlungsod for their disposition. You will be notified (text or phone call) when to return for the action of the Sangguniang Panlungsod, after which the dropping certification is processed and released.

## Requirements

### For New / Renewal / Unit Substitution / Transfer of Franchise

- [ ] Duly notarized verified petition (1 original copy)
- [ ] Voter's Certification as a registered voter in the City of Koronadal (1 original copy)
- [ ] Any one proof of identification (e.g. utility billing statement in the petitioner's name, National ID, Postal ID, Senior Citizen ID, PWD ID, or Certificate of Title) — 1 photocopy with 3 signature specimens
- [ ] Barangay Clearance where the operator resides / tricycle garage is located (1 original copy)
- [ ] Certificate of Registration (CR) and Official Receipt (OR) of the motorcycle, duly registered at LTO – Koronadal District (1 photocopy each)
- [ ] Certificate of Membership (1 original copy)
- [ ] Sketch map and location of garage/residence with complete landmark (1 copy)
- [ ] Driver's license of the tricycle driver/s, plus proof of residency and authorization to drive from the operator (1 copy each)
- [ ] Pictures of the tricycle (4R size), clear and full view — one picture each of the motorcycle and the sidecar's front and back
- [ ] Picture of the garage (4R size), clear and full view (1 copy)

**Additional — for Transfer of Franchise:** Extra Judicial Partition with Waiver of Rights, or Deed of Donation (within the second degree of consanguinity or affinity only) — 1 copy.

**Additional — for Unit Substitution:** OR and CR of the old tricycle unit (registered as private use only) and OR and CR of the new tricycle unit (registered as motorcycle with sidecar) — 1 photocopy each.

> The charter also lists special supplementary requirements for operators working abroad, teachers/military personnel, cases involving the death of the owner/operator, and operators under medication. Ask the Franchising Section if any of these apply to you.

### For Securing the Approved MTOP (claiming step)

- [ ] Notice of Hearing
- [ ] Certificate of Completion (present original copy — from the City Traffic Office)
- [ ] OR and CR of the tricycle unit registered as motorcycle with sidecar (1 photocopy each)

### For Annual MTOP Compliance

- [ ] Latest approved MTOP (1 original or 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Inspection Slip with remarks "PASSED" (1 original)
- [ ] Duly registered OR and CR of tricycle ownership (1 original or 1 photocopy)

### For Dropping an MTOP

- [ ] Letter of Intent (1 original)
- [ ] Latest approved MTOP (1 original or 1 photocopy)
- [ ] Duly registered OR and CR of tricycle ownership (1 original or 1 photocopy)

### Eligibility

- Tricycle owners/operators who are bonafide residents of the City of Koronadal (for new, renewal, substitution, and transfer).
- Existing MTOP holders (for annual compliance and dropping).

## Fees

**New / Renewal / Unit Substitution / Transfer of Franchise**

- MTOP Certification Fee: PhP 300.00
- Documentary Stamp Tax: PhP 30.00
- Franchise Fee (new and transfer of franchise only): PhP 350.00
- Total: PhP 330.00, or PhP 680.00 if the franchise fee applies.

**Annual MTOP Compliance**

- Annual Inspection Fee with Sticker: PhP 200.00
- Annual Occupational Permit Fee with ID: PhP 200.00
- Annual Environmental Management Fee: PhP 100.00
- Total: PhP 500.00

**Dropping an MTOP**

- Processing fee: PhP 250.00
- Certification Fee: PhP 100.00 plus Documentary Stamp Tax: PhP 30.00 (charged at the certification step)

Late filing of a renewal (less than one month before expiry) is subject to penalties. Unpaid annual regulatory fees from previous years are collected before an MTOP can be renewed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When should I renew my MTOP?**
A: At least one (1) month before the expiration date. Filing later than that is considered late filing and is subject to penalties.

**Q: Do I always pay the franchise fee?**
A: No. The PhP 350.00 franchise fee is charged only for new applications and transfers of franchise. Renewals do not pay it.

**Q: When is the annual compliance done?**
A: Every first quarter of the year. Your tricycle must pass inspection and you must pay the annual fees as continuing requirements for tricycle-for-hire operation. Unpaid fees from previous years are collected before renewal.

**Q: Where do I get the Certificate of Completion needed to claim my approved MTOP?**
A: From the City Traffic Office — present the original copy at the Franchising Section.

**Q: I want to stop operating. What do I do?**
A: File for dropping of your MTOP. You must settle all outstanding fees and charges at the City Treasurer's Office first, then submit a Letter of Intent with your MTOP and tricycle OR/CR.
