# How to Get a Barangay Clearance — City of Koronadal

This guide helps residents obtain a Barangay Clearance from their local Barangay Hall — a common requirement for business permits, employment, and various transactions.

---

## Who Needs This

A Barangay Clearance is issued by the barangay where you reside or where your business is located — **not** at City Hall. You may need one if you are:

- Applying for or renewing a business permit (a Barangay Business Clearance is usually required first)
- Applying for employment or job requirements
- Processing loans, IDs, or other government and private transactions that require proof of good standing in the community

---

## Requirements

Typical requirements include:

- Proof of residency (e.g., utility bill, lease contract, or other proof of address)
- Valid government-issued ID
- Statement of the purpose of the clearance
- Cedula / Community Tax Certificate (often required)
- For business clearance: business details such as name, location, and nature of business

---

## Step-by-Step

1. Go to the Barangay Hall of the barangay where you live or where your business is located.
2. Inform the barangay staff of the purpose of your clearance.
3. Fill out the request form and submit your requirements.
4. Pay the corresponding barangay fee.
5. Wait for processing and claim your Barangay Clearance.

---

## Fees and Timeline

Barangay Clearances usually involve a small fee that varies per barangay and depending on the purpose — confirm the current amount with your Barangay Hall. Most clearances are processed on the same day once your requirements are complete.

---

## Where to Go

**Your Barangay Hall** (the barangay where you reside or where your business is located).
