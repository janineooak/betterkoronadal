# City Ordinances of Koronadal

A reference list of ordinances enacted by the Sangguniang Panlungsod (City Council) of Koronadal, organized by year and mirroring the City's official ordinance archive.

Ordinances are local laws passed by the City Council. The list below is provided for quick reference. For the full, signed text of any ordinance, consult the Sangguniang Panlungsod Office or the official archive at https://koronadal.gov.ph/ordinance/.

## 2022

The City Council enacted the following measures in 2022 (refer to the official archive for exact ordinance numbers and text):

- Loan agreement with the Land Bank of the Philippines for motorpool expansion (₱111.5 million)
- Amendment to the "Bulig sa Kubos" program (Ordinance No. 28, S-2021)
- Amendment to the Solo Parent Welfare Ordinance (Ordinance No. 1, S-2020)
- Renaming the City Public Market to the Central Supermarket Complex
- Birthday cash gift of ₱1,000 for senior citizens aged 60–84
- Term loan with the Development Bank of the Philippines for public market construction (₱300 million)
- Creating the Motorized Tricycle Franchising Regulatory Board
- Expanding tricycle registration and application requirements
- Amendment to Ordinance No. 11, Series of 2009
- "The New Isko City Scholarship Program 2022"
