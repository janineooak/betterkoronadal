# City Traffic & Management Office — City of Koronadal

The City Traffic & Management Office keeps Koronadal's roads safe and moving by managing traffic flow, enforcing road safety, maintaining signage, and ensuring the orderly movement of vehicles and pedestrians.

## Mandate and Functions

In support of the city's mandate under the Local Government Code (RA 7160) and traffic management ordinances, the City Traffic & Management Office is responsible for:

- Managing and regulating traffic flow on city roads and intersections.
- Enforcing traffic laws, local traffic ordinances, and road safety regulations.
- Installing and maintaining traffic signs, markings, and signal devices.
- Ensuring the orderly movement of vehicles and the safety of pedestrians.
- Coordinating with the PNP and other agencies on road safety and traffic enforcement.
- Conducting road safety education and managing traffic during events and emergencies.

---

## Services Offered

- Traffic enforcement and management on city roads and key intersections.
- Installation and upkeep of traffic signs, road markings, and pedestrian facilities.
- Coordination on traffic rerouting and management during events, road works, and emergencies.
- Road safety awareness and education activities for drivers and pedestrians.
- Response to reports of traffic hazards, obstructions, and unsafe road conditions.

---

## How to Reach This Office

1. Visit the City Traffic & Management Office at the New City Hall Grounds during regular office hours.
2. Describe your concern, such as a traffic hazard report, signage request, or assistance for an event.
3. Provide the location details and any documents requested by the office.
4. Follow the office's guidance on coordination and next steps.
