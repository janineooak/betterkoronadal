# City Economic Enterprise Development Office (CEEDO) — City of Koronadal

The City Economic Enterprise Development Office (CEEDO) manages the city's revenue-generating facilities — such as the public markets, transport terminals, and slaughterhouse — to keep them well-run, sanitary, and beneficial to the public.

## Mandate and Functions

The City Economic Enterprise Development Office oversees the operation and development of the city's economic enterprises and revenue-generating facilities. Its core functions include:

- Managing and operating the city's economic enterprises, including public markets, transport terminals, and the slaughterhouse.
- Administering the lease and assignment of market stalls, booths, and other commercial spaces owned by the city.
- Ensuring the cleanliness, orderliness, safety, and sanitation of city-run facilities.
- Developing programs and improvements to make city enterprises more efficient, viable, and self-sustaining.
- Coordinating with the Treasurer's Office on the collection of market fees, stall rentals, terminal fees, and other charges.
- Enforcing the rules and regulations governing vendors, concessionaires, and users of city facilities.
- Recommending policies and rates for the city's economic enterprises to the local government.

---

## Services Offered

- Application and assignment of public market stalls and commercial spaces.
- Renewal and transfer of stall or space privileges, subject to city rules.
- Management of transport terminal operations and assignment of terminal spaces.
- Administration of slaughterhouse operations and related services.
- Coordination on the payment of stall rentals, market fees, and terminal fees.
- Handling of concerns, requests, and complaints from vendors and facility users.

> Rates, requirements, and the availability of stalls or spaces may change. Please confirm details with the office before applying.

---

## How to Reach This Office

1. Visit the CEEDO at the New City Hall Grounds during regular weekday office hours.
2. Vendors and concessionaires may coordinate here for stall applications, renewals, and facility concerns.
3. For inquiries about requirements, availability, or fees, call the City Hall trunkline.
