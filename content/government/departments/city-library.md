# City Library — City of Koronadal

The City Library provides Koronadal residents and students with free public access to books, references, internet and learning resources, and reading programs that support education and lifelong learning.

## Mandate and Functions

As a public library of the City Government of Koronadal, the City Library is responsible for:

- Providing free public access to books, references, and reading materials for residents of all ages.
- Offering a quiet space for study, research, and reading for students and the community.
- Maintaining and developing the library's collection of print and digital resources.
- Providing internet and learning resources to support education and research.
- Conducting reading, literacy, and learning programs for students and residents.
- Assisting library users in finding and using information resources.
- Coordinating with the National Library of the Philippines and partner institutions.

---

## Services Offered

- In-library reading and reference services for books, periodicals, and other materials.
- Study and research space for students and residents.
- Internet access and computer-assisted learning resources, where available.
- Reading, literacy, and storytelling programs for children and students.
- Research assistance and guidance from library staff.
- Borrowing services for registered library members, subject to library rules.

---

## How to Avail of Services

1. Visit the City Library at the New City Hall Grounds during library hours.
2. Register as a library user or member if required for the service you need.
3. Browse the collection or ask the staff for assistance in finding materials.
4. Use the materials and resources in line with the library's rules and policies.
