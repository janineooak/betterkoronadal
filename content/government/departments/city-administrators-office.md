## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the City Administrator's Office is responsible for:

- Developing and recommending plans, programmes, and strategies to assist the City Mayor in governing the city.
- Coordinating, supervising, and overseeing the work of all city departments and offices to ensure efficient and effective service delivery.
- Assisting in the preparation of the executive and legislative agenda and the annual budget proposals of the city.
- Establishing and maintaining sound internal systems and controls for the management of city operations.
- Ensuring that programmes, projects, and activities of the city are implemented in accordance with approved plans and policies.
- Acting on administrative and operational matters as delegated by the City Mayor.

---

## Services Offered

- Overall coordination and supervision of city departments and offices.
- Review and endorsement of inter-office concerns, projects, and policy recommendations to the Mayor.
- Assistance in administrative matters, complaints, and requests directed to the Office of the Mayor.
- Support in the preparation, monitoring, and evaluation of city programmes and projects.
- Facilitation of coordination between the City Government and other agencies, partners, and stakeholders.
- Guidance on internal procedures and administrative requirements for transacting offices.
