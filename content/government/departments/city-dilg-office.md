# City DILG Office — City of Koronadal

The City DILG Office is the field presence of the Department of the Interior and Local Government in Koronadal, supporting good local governance and assisting the city's 27 barangays in meeting national standards and compliance requirements.

## Mandate and Functions

As the DILG field office, the City DILG Office is responsible for:

- Providing oversight, technical assistance, and capacity-building support to the City Government and its 27 barangays.
- Promoting good local governance and ensuring local compliance with national laws, DILG issuances, and policies.
- Monitoring the performance of local government units through tools such as the Seal of Good Local Governance (SGLG).
- Supporting peace and order, public safety, and disaster-resilience programmes at the local level.
- Assisting in the orientation and continuing education of barangay officials and functionaries.
- Coordinating the implementation of DILG and national government programmes within the city.

---

## Services Offered

- Technical assistance and advisory services to barangay officials and city offices on local governance.
- Facilitation of barangay-related concerns, including dispute resolution (Katarungang Pambarangay) guidance.
- Assistance with DILG programmes, performance assessments, and compliance documentation.
- Coordination on peace and order, public safety, and anti-illegal-drugs initiatives.
- Capacity-building and orientation sessions for newly elected and incumbent local officials.
- Endorsement and processing of certifications and requirements within DILG's mandate.
