# Invite the Mayor to Your Event

Residents, organizations, and institutions may invite the Mayor to attend official community activities such as:

- community celebrations
- school programs
- civic ceremonies
- government events

To request the Mayor's attendance, please submit a formal invitation to the **Office of the City Mayor**.

Required information:

- Name of organization
- Event title
- Date and time
- Event location
- Contact person and details

# About the City of Koronadal

The **{GOVERNMENT_NAME}** — also known as **Marbel** — is the capital of the Province of **South Cotabato** and the regional center of **SOCCSKSARGEN (Region XII)**. Often called the **"Hub of the South,"** Koronadal is home to more than **201,000 residents** across **27 barangays**.

The Office of the City Mayor plays a vital role in guiding the city's development, including:

- implementing local development plans
- overseeing city government operations
- managing emergency and disaster response
- representing the city in regional and national engagements
