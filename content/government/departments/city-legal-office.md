# City Legal Office — City of Koronadal

The City Legal Office provides legal advice to city officials and offices, drafts and reviews legal documents, and represents the City Government of Koronadal in legal matters.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the City Legal Office is responsible for:

- Providing legal advice and opinions to the City Mayor, the Sangguniang Panlungsod, and all city offices on official matters.
- Representing the City Government in civil actions and proceedings where the city or its officials are a party in their official capacity.
- Drafting and reviewing ordinances, resolutions, contracts, memoranda of agreement, and other legal instruments.
- Investigating and recommending action on administrative cases involving city personnel, as directed.
- Ensuring that the official acts of the city conform with existing laws, rules, and regulations.
- Rendering opinions on the legality of proposed transactions and the validity of legal documents.

---

## Services Offered

- Legal opinions and advisory services for city officials and departments.
- Drafting, review, and notarisation (when applicable) of contracts and legal documents for the city.
- Legal representation of the City Government in court and quasi-judicial proceedings.
- Review of ordinances, resolutions, and other legislative measures for legal soundness.
- Assistance and recommendations on administrative cases involving city personnel.
- Guidance to other offices on compliance with laws and regulations.
