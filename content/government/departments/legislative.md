# Sangguniang Panlungsod (City Council)

The **Sangguniang Panlungsod** is the legislative body of the **City of Koronadal**. It enacts ordinances, approves resolutions, and adopts the policies and the annual budget that guide the city's development. The **Vice Mayor** serves as the Presiding Officer of the Council.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the Sangguniang Panlungsod is responsible for:

- Enacting ordinances and approving resolutions for the general welfare of the city
- Reviewing and approving the **annual and supplemental budgets**
- Levying taxes, fees, and charges within the limits set by law
- Acting on franchises, permits, and the use of city property
- Reviewing the ordinances of component barangays

## Attend a Council Session

Regular sessions of the Sangguniang Panlungsod are generally open to the public. Residents who wish to observe a session, file a request, or follow up on a proposed ordinance or resolution may coordinate with the **Office of the Secretary to the Sanggunian**.
