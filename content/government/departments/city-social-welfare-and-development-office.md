# City Social Welfare & Development Office (CSWDO) — City of Koronadal

The City Social Welfare & Development Office provides social protection and assistance to vulnerable residents of Koronadal, including senior citizens, persons with disabilities, solo parents, children, women, and indigent families.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the City Social Welfare & Development Office is responsible for:

- Formulating and implementing social welfare programs that protect and uplift disadvantaged and vulnerable sectors.
- Providing direct assistance and case management for indigent individuals and families in crisis.
- Implementing programs for senior citizens, persons with disabilities (PWDs), solo parents, children, and women.
- Conducting community-based social services, including child protection and family welfare.
- Coordinating disaster-related relief and social services with the City DRRM Office during emergencies.
- Referring clients to livelihood, health, legal, and other support services as needed.

---

## Services Offered

- Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situation (AICS), including medical, burial, and financial aid for qualified residents.
- Issuance and processing of senior citizen, PWD, and solo parent identification and benefits.
- Case management and counseling for children, women, and families in difficult circumstances.
- Child protection and welfare services, including support for children in need of special protection.
- Livelihood program referrals and assistance for indigent families.
- Relief assistance and social services for families affected by disasters or emergencies.

---

## How to Avail of Services

1. Visit the City Social Welfare & Development Office during office hours.
2. Approach the intake or social worker's desk and explain your situation or the assistance you need.
3. Submit the requirements (such as a valid ID and supporting documents) requested by the assigned social worker.
4. Undergo assessment or interview, then follow up on the schedule or referral provided.
