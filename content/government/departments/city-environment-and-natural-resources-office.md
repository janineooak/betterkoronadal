# City Environment & Natural Resources Office (CENRO) — City of Koronadal

The City Environment & Natural Resources Office protects Koronadal's environment through solid waste management, tree planting, and enforcement of environmental rules, working to keep the city clean, green, and sustainable for residents.

## Mandate and Functions

In line with the Local Government Code (RA 7160) and national environmental laws, the City Environment & Natural Resources Office is responsible for:

- Implementing programs for environmental protection, conservation, and the sustainable use of natural resources.
- Managing solid waste collection, segregation, and disposal under the Ecological Solid Waste Management Act (RA 9003).
- Leading tree planting, greening, and ecosystem rehabilitation activities across the city.
- Enforcing environmental ordinances and applicable national environmental laws and regulations.
- Coordinating with national agencies, barangays, and stakeholders on environmental concerns.
- Promoting environmental awareness and community participation in conservation efforts.

---

## Services Offered

- Coordination and support for solid waste collection, segregation, and proper disposal.
- Seedling distribution and support for tree planting and greening activities.
- Receiving and acting on reports of environmental violations and pollution concerns.
- Environmental education and information drives for schools, barangays, and the public.
- Technical assistance on environmental compliance for projects and establishments.
