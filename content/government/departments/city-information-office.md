## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the City Information Office is responsible for:

- Providing the City Mayor with information and advice on public information matters and the city's public image.
- Developing and implementing plans and programmes for an effective public information and communication system.
- Disseminating accurate and timely information on city programmes, projects, services, and policies to residents.
- Maintaining the city's official communication channels, including its website and social media pages.
- Coordinating with media organisations and managing press releases, advisories, and public announcements.
- Documenting and publicising city events, activities, and achievements.

---

## Services Offered

- Release of official public advisories, announcements, and emergency information.
- Publication of news and updates on city programmes, projects, and services.
- Coverage and documentation of city events and activities (photos and stories).
- Coordination with local and regional media for press releases and interviews.
- Management of the city's official website and social media platforms.
- Assistance to other city offices in preparing public-facing information materials.
