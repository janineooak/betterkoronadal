# City Planning & Development Office (CPDO) — City of Koronadal

The City Planning & Development Office leads the city's long-term planning, preparing the development and land use plans that guide where Koronadal grows and how public resources are invested for residents.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the City Planning & Development Office is responsible for:

- Formulating and updating the city's Comprehensive Land Use Plan (CLUP) and zoning ordinance recommendations.
- Preparing the Comprehensive Development Plan (CDP) and the local development investment program.
- Maintaining and updating the city's socio-economic profile and other planning databases.
- Conducting research and providing technical and economic analysis to guide development decisions.
- Coordinating with the City Development Council and assisting in the integration of barangay and sectoral plans.
- Monitoring and evaluating the implementation of development programs, projects, and activities.

---

## Services Offered

- Issuance of zoning certifications and locational clearances in line with the approved land use plan.
- Provision of city statistics, maps, and socio-economic data for researchers, students, and partner agencies.
- Technical assistance to barangays and other offices in preparing plans and project proposals.
- Review and endorsement of development proposals for consistency with the CLUP and zoning ordinance.
- Support to the City Development Council in plan preparation and project prioritization.
