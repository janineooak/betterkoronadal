# City Budget Office — City of Koronadal

The City Budget Office prepares, manages, and monitors the city's annual budget, making sure that public funds are planned and allocated to the programs and services that benefit the people of Koronadal.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code of 1991 (RA 7160), the City Budget Office is responsible for the preparation and administration of the city budget. Its core functions include:

- Preparing forms, orders, and circulars embodying instructions on budgetary and appropriation matters for the local chief executive's approval.
- Assisting the local chief executive in the preparation of the annual budget and supplemental budgets.
- Reviewing and consolidating the budget proposals of the different departments and offices of the city.
- Controlling and monitoring the release and use of appropriations against approved budgets.
- Preparing allotment and obligation records to ensure spending stays within authorized limits.
- Coordinating with the Treasurer, Accounting, and Planning offices on income estimates and fund availability.
- Submitting periodic budget accountability and utilization reports.

---

## Services Offered

- Technical assistance to departments and offices in preparing their budget proposals.
- Review and consolidation of the proposed annual and supplemental budgets.
- Certification and control of appropriations and allotments.
- Monitoring of budget utilization and obligations of city offices.
- Preparation of budget accountability reports.
- Guidance on budgetary rules, circulars, and procedures for city offices and barangays.
