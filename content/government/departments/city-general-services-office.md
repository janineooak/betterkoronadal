# City General Services Office (CGSO) — City of Koronadal

The City General Services Office handles the procurement of supplies, management of city property and records, and the upkeep of City Government of Koronadal facilities so that other offices can operate smoothly.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the City General Services Office is responsible for:

- Taking custody of and being accountable for all city properties, real or personal, not otherwise assigned to other offices.
- Managing the procurement, storage, and distribution of supplies, materials, and equipment for city offices.
- Maintaining and safekeeping the records of supplies, property, and equipment of the city.
- Overseeing the repair, maintenance, and upkeep of city buildings, facilities, and other physical assets.
- Administering the city's records management and archives system.
- Supervising general services such as janitorial, security, and utilities support for city facilities.

---

## Services Offered

- Procurement and supply of office materials, equipment, and other requirements for city offices.
- Inventory, recording, and disposal of city property and equipment.
- Maintenance and repair of city buildings, facilities, and utilities.
- Records management, archiving, and document safekeeping services.
- Coordination of janitorial, security, and other general support services.
- Assistance to city offices in property accountability and supply requests.

## How to Reach This Office

1. Visit the City General Services Office at the New City Hall during regular weekday office hours (Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM).
2. Call the City Hall trunkline at (083) 228-6095 to coordinate supply, property, or maintenance requests.
3. Submit the required forms or requests through your office and follow up with the CGSO, or email info.koronadalcity@gmail.com.
