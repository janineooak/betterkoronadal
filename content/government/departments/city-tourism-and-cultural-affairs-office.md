The City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office promotes Koronadal as a destination, supports the city's festivals and events, and safeguards the local culture and heritage of the "Hub of the South."

## Mandate and Functions

As provided under the Local Government Code (RA 7160) and tourism laws, the City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office is responsible for:

- Promoting Koronadal as a tourism, business, and events destination within SOCCSKSARGEN (Region XII).
- Planning and supporting the city's festivals and celebrations, including the city fiesta and Hinugyaw Festival.
- Safeguarding and promoting local culture, traditions, and heritage, including the heritage of Koronadal's indigenous and settler communities.
- Developing and improving tourism sites, products, and experiences in the city.
- Assisting tourists, visitors, and tour groups with information and coordination.
- Supporting cultural workers, artists, and community groups in their activities.
- Coordinating with the Department of Tourism and other agencies on tourism programs.

---

## Services Offered

- Tourist information, assistance, and travel guidance for visitors to Koronadal.
- Coordination and support for festivals, cultural shows, and city events.
- Promotion of local attractions, products, and tourism experiences.
- Support and accreditation assistance for tourism establishments, as applicable.
- Cultural and heritage programs for residents, students, and community groups.
- Assistance to artists, performers, and cultural organizations.

---

## How to Avail of Services

1. Visit the City Tourism & Cultural Affairs Office at the New City Hall Grounds or contact it through the City Hall trunkline.
2. State your concern, such as a tourism inquiry, event support, or cultural program participation.
3. Provide any details or documents requested for your specific concern.
4. Coordinate with the office staff for scheduling, assistance, or next steps.
