# City Engineering Office — City of Koronadal

The City Engineering Office plans, builds, and maintains Koronadal's roads, bridges, drainage, and public buildings, and supports the issuance of building and occupancy permits to keep construction safe and orderly.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the City Engineering Office is responsible for:

- Planning, designing, and supervising the construction and maintenance of city roads, bridges, and drainage systems.
- Constructing and maintaining public buildings and other city infrastructure.
- Providing engineering services and technical advice to the City Mayor and the Sanggunian.
- Supporting the issuance of building and occupancy permits in coordination with the Office of the Building Official.
- Conducting inspections to ensure compliance with the National Building Code and engineering standards.
- Preparing engineering estimates, programs of work, and project documentation for city infrastructure.

---

## Services Offered

- Assistance with building and occupancy permit applications in coordination with the Office of the Building Official.
- Inspection of construction projects for compliance with the National Building Code.
- Maintenance and repair of city roads, drainage, and public buildings.
- Preparation of engineering plans, estimates, and programs of work for city projects.
- Technical evaluation and supervision of infrastructure projects.

---

## How to Reach This Office

1. Visit the City Engineering Office at the New City Hall Grounds during regular office hours.
2. State the permit assistance, inspection request, or technical service you need.
3. Submit the required plans and documents and settle any applicable fees at the City Treasurer's Office.
4. Coordinate with the office on inspection schedules and processing timelines.
