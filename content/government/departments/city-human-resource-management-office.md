# City Human Resource Management Office — City of Koronadal

The City Human Resource Management Office manages the recruitment, welfare, and development of the City Government of Koronadal's workforce so that the city can deliver quality public service.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Local Government Code (RA 7160) and Civil Service rules, the City Human Resource Management Office is responsible for:

- Developing and implementing the city's human resource plans, policies, and programmes.
- Managing recruitment, selection, appointment, and placement of city personnel in accordance with merit and fitness.
- Administering personnel records, attendance, leave, payroll-related personnel data, and benefits.
- Implementing learning and development, training, and capacity-building programmes for employees.
- Administering performance management, rewards and recognition, and employee welfare programmes.
- Ensuring compliance with Civil Service Commission (CSC) rules and regulations.

---

## Services Offered

- Posting of job vacancies and processing of applications and appointments.
- Issuance of certificates of employment, service records, and other personnel certifications.
- Processing of leave applications and other personnel benefit transactions.
- Coordination of employee training, seminars, and development programmes.
- Administration of performance evaluation and rewards and recognition programmes.
- Assistance to employees on HR-related concerns, policies, and welfare matters.
