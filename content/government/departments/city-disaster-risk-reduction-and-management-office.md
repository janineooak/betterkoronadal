The City Disaster Risk Reduction & Management Office leads Koronadal's disaster preparedness, early warning, emergency response, and evacuation efforts, and operates the Korona-911 / 911 emergency hotline.

## Mandate and Functions

Under the Philippine Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Act (RA 10121) and the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the CDRRMO is responsible for:

- Formulating and implementing the City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Plan.
- Leading disaster preparedness, mitigation, and prevention activities across the city.
- Operating early warning systems and disseminating advisories to residents and barangays.
- Coordinating and leading emergency response, search and rescue, and evacuation operations.
- Operating the Korona-911 / 911 emergency hotline for round-the-clock emergency response.
- Managing evacuation centers and coordinating relief operations during disasters.
- Conducting drills, training, and capacity-building for communities, schools, and barangay response teams.

---

## Services Offered

- Korona-911 / 911 emergency hotline for fire, medical, accident, and disaster emergencies.
- Emergency response, ambulance, and search and rescue assistance.
- Early warning advisories and weather and hazard information.
- Disaster preparedness training, drills, and seminars for communities and institutions.
- Coordination of evacuation and relief operations during calamities.
- Hazard and risk information to support community safety planning.

---

## How to Avail of Services

1. For emergencies, call the **Korona-911 / 911** hotline immediately and clearly state your location and the nature of the emergency.
2. For training, drills, or preparedness assistance, visit or contact the CDRRMO during office hours.
3. Coordinate with your barangay or barangay emergency response team for community-level concerns.
4. Follow official advisories and instructions from the CDRRMO during disasters and emergencies.
