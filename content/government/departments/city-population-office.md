The City Population Office manages Koronadal's population program, promoting responsible parenthood, family planning, and adolescent health services for residents and families.

## Mandate and Functions

In line with the national population and development program and the Local Government Code (RA 7160), the City Population Office is responsible for:

- Implementing the city's population and development program in coordination with national agencies.
- Promoting responsible parenthood and family planning information and services.
- Conducting information, education, and communication (IEC) activities on population and reproductive health.
- Implementing adolescent health and youth development programs.
- Coordinating with barangays and health workers to deliver population services at the community level.
- Maintaining population data to support city planning and development.

---

## Services Offered

- Responsible parenthood and family planning counseling and referrals.
- Information and education sessions on reproductive health and population concerns.
- Adolescent health and youth development programs and seminars.
- Pre-marriage counseling and orientation for couples.
- Community-based population and development activities in barangays.
- Referrals to the City Health Office and partner agencies for clinical family planning services.

---

## How to Avail of Services

1. Visit the City Population Office during office hours, or coordinate through your barangay.
2. Inform the staff of the program or counseling service you wish to avail.
3. Attend the scheduled counseling, orientation, or seminar.
4. Follow any referral provided for clinical or health-related services.
