# Population and Demographics

An overview of the population of the City of Koronadal based on Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) census data, including population over time and population density.

The City of Koronadal had a population of 201,844 according to the 2024 PSA census, up from 195,398 in the 2020 PSA census. As the capital of South Cotabato and the regional center of SOCCSKSARGEN, it is among the most populous urban centers in the region.

## Population Density

Based on a land area of 277.00 km², the city's population density is approximately **729 persons/km²** (derived: 201,844 ÷ 277.00 km²).

---

## Regional Role

Koronadal serves as the capital of the Province of South Cotabato and as the regional center of SOCCSKSARGEN (Region XII). This dual role makes the city a focal point for provincial and regional administration, services, and commerce in the southern part of Mindanao.
