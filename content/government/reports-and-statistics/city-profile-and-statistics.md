# City Profile and Statistics

A quick-reference profile of the City of Koronadal, presenting key facts and statistics about the city's identity, geography, and history at a glance.

The City of Koronadal, also known as Marbel, is a component city and the capital of the Province of South Cotabato. It serves as the regional center of SOCCSKSARGEN (Region XII) and carries the nickname "Hub of the South."

## Geography

Koronadal occupies a land area of 277.00 km² in the Province of South Cotabato, in the southern part of Mindanao. As the capital of the province and the regional center of SOCCSKSARGEN (Region XII), it functions as a key administrative and service hub for the surrounding area. The city is divided into 27 barangays.

## Etymology

The name "Koronadal" is derived from two Blaan words: "koron" (cogon grass) and "nadal" (wide plain). Together they describe the wide, cogon-covered plain on which the city sits. The city is also widely known by its earlier name, Marbel.
