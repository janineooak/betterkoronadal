# Barangay Directory

A directory of the 27 barangays that make up the City of Koronadal, the capital of South Cotabato.

The City of Koronadal is composed of 27 barangays. This directory lists all of them in alphabetical order for easy reference.

---

## Most Populous Barangays

The most populous barangays in the city are **General Paulino Santos**, **Santa Cruz**, **Santo Niño**, **Zone III**, and **Zulueta**.
