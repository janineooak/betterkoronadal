## City profile

- **Official name:** City of Koronadal (also known as **Marbel**)
- **Type:** Component city
- **Capital of:** Province of South Cotabato
- **Regional center of:** SOCCSKSARGEN (Region XII)
- **Motto:** "One People, One Big Dream, One Koronadal!"
- **Nickname:** "Hub of the South"
- **Population:** 201,844 (2024 PSA census; 195,398 in 2020)
- **Land area:** 277.00 km²
- **Barangays:** 27 (largest: Gen. Paulino Santos, Santa Cruz, Sto. Niño, Zone III, Zulueta)
- **Etymology:** Blaan "koron" (cogon grass) + "nadal" (wide plain)
- **ZIP:** 9506
- **History:** Created as municipality of Marbel via EO 82 (Aug 18, 1947); became
  South Cotabato capital via RA 4849 (Jul 18, 1966); converted to component city
  via RA 8803 (Oct 8, 2000).
